I am a fresh diploma graduate from Nanyang Polytechnic who specialises in Cybersecurity and Digital Forensics.

When presented with something new, we as humans like to speculate and create hypotheses. It is the most natural way of learning outside of our comfort zones. I love to be proven wrong and to learn about certain behaviours.

I believe that as our modern society evolves into an even more tech-savvy society, we can use big data to our advantage to capitalise our consumer needs and better understand unique behaviours.

Why pick me?

  • 01 I am a headstrong learner who is not afraid of failures.

    I might make mistakes, but it is important to learn from them!

  • 02 I am very organised and focused on the nitty-gritty details in everything I do.

    I believe part of being a Data Analyst involves being precise and scrutinising everything that crosses their path. I have much admiration for their efforts and open-mindedness in their interpretation. Statistics can often tell us something out of the ordinary, such as the outliers. It is easy to always turn a blind eye on outliers in terms of graphical interpretations. However, outliers also tell a different story for being an outlier.

  • 03 I value teamwork over individual contributions.

    Teamwork makes the dream work! Having a team around boosts team morale and can even spawn new, creative ideas and different interpretations. Data is often very varied, making different calls is important to see the big picture.

 

Projects

SWOY Bubble Tea

A web-based application that needs security hardening.
For this project, our team was tasked to create two versions of a website, an insecure and a secure version.
We were to choose 2 vulnerabilities from the OWASP Top 10 Web Vulnerabilities and provide a solution to fix the 2 vulnerabilities we have chosen.
I have chosen to work on Insufficient Logging and Monitoring, and Broken Authentication.

Applied Cryptography Project

A simple website that allows user to learn and understand how certain cryptographic techniques work.
It features encryption and decryption tools from different cryptographic methods such as Rail Fence, Vernam Cipher, AES, etc.
It also offers some visualisation and explanations on how different cryptographic methods work.

Delorem Ipsum Drug Store
